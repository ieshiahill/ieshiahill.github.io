Who am I?

I am creator, a servant and a doer.

Creator



Ever since I was child, I've always had the desire to create. I enjoyed sketching shoes, Looney Toons characters and landscapes. I always have a pencil and paper in hand and I would sketch whenever I had the opportunity. When I was nine years old, I sketched my first logo and it's my personal logo that I still use to this day.

As an adult, my creativity transferred over into creating businesses, helping friends expand on their business ideas creating art and designing and developing web and mobile applications.

Servant



I know my purpose is to serve others. Whether it's helping a family member or friend, volunteering my time to positively impact inner-city children, helping young girls build their self-confidence through sports and helping resolve customer issues.

Doer



I know how to get things done. Period. I've experienced a lot of obstacles in life and as cliche as it may sound, I strongly believe that through my faith, I can do anything that I put my mind to. I set goals and I crush them, no matter how challenging or long it may take.

My favorite aspect of this role was I able to listen to the pains of my key customers and create solutions. This normally entailed working with the software development team to create add or modify features on the software system and the mobile app my customers used. It was my job to ensure that their experience using our software Eleven Fifty Academy, I discovered User Experience Design which covered all of my strengths and passions by allowing me to combine my business, tech and creative skills to create user-centered digital solutions that positively impacted business goals as well.
