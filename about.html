I am creator, a servant and a doer.

Creator

Ever since I was child, I've always had the desire to create. I always have a pencil and paper in hand and I would sketch whenever I had the opportunity. As a kid I enjoyed drawing shoes, Looney Toons characters and landscapes. When I was nine years old, I sketched my first logo and it's my personal logo that I still use to this day.

As an adult, my creativity transferred over into more creative problem solving from starting businesses, helping friends expand on their business ideas, creating art, and designing and developing web and mobile applications as well.

Servant

My purpose is to serve others. Whether it's helping a stranger, a family member or friend, volunteering my time to positively impact the lives of inner-city children, helping young girls build their self-confidence through sports and improve customer experiences in corprate America.

Doer

I know how to get things done. Period. As cliche as it may sound, I strongly believe that with faith and action, I can do anything that I put my mind to. I set goals and I crush them, no matter how challenging the goal may be.

My Career Journey

I spent my 20's figuring out my strengths and my weaknesses and discovering my true passions. I've worked in roles writing and editing content, customer service, sales and account management and I think I've finally figured my strengths and things I enjoy doing everyday.

In my previous role as account manager, I managed a portfolio of over 100 key customers. I throughly enjoyed this role because I learned so much! I was able to foster relationships with key executives and stakeholders from all over the country, I helped them solve their business problems, I was able to learn from my CEO and other salesman who held decades of experience and I was able to truly learn how a business operations truly work. My favorite aspect of this role was that I able to listen to the pains of my key customers and create solutions that would improve their entire customer experience. This frequently entailed working with the software development team to create or modify features on the software system and the mobile app. It was my job to ensure that their experience using our software improved which in return would increase the revenue allowing me to hit my sales goals.
In this role, I was able to collaborte with the development team, participate in QA sessions and witness digital products go from an idea to launch. I soon decided I wanted to become more hands on with creating software products so I decided to explore a career in tech.
I became a student at the Eleven Fifty Academy where I participated in an intensive 12-week JavaScript coding bootcamp. During my studies, I discovered User Experience Design and I immediately fell in love because it covered all of my strengths and passions by allowing me to combine my business, tech and creative skills to create user-centered digital solutions that positively impacted business goals as well.


I am excited share my UX designer journey with you all and if you would like to connect, feel free to contact me at hello@ieshiahill.com
